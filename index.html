Fun Facts about Belgium

Belgium holds the world record for the longest period without a government. That is because we are notoriously difficult to deal with.

Belgium produces more than 220,000 tons of chocolate per annum. Yet we are still not the most obese country in the world... yet!

Robert Cailliau, co-inventor of the World Wide Web, is a Belgian. If only he could see how far I've come.

Our national symbol is a little peeing boy. I can't even make jokes about it, that's just how it is.
